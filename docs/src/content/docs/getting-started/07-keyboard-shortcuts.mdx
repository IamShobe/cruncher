## Tips

- **Re-evaluate vs Full Search**: Use `Shift+Enter` to quickly re-evaluate your pipeline after modifying a command. Use `Cmd+Shift+Enter` to perform a full search (which may reset the time range).
- **Query Editor Focus**: Press `Alt+Q` to quickly jump to the query editor if you're in another part of the UI.
- **Time Range**: Press `Alt+T` to open the time range picker without typing.
- **Share Queries**: Use `Cmd+Shift+C` to copy a shareable link to your current search.
